About Us...

We are a diverse group of people joined by a common interest in honest food and honoring our culture, traditions, environment, and community

We organize many activities: neighborhood walks, food and wine tastings, cooking demonstrations, and educational projects, including the "Edible Schoolyard" at the Dawes Elementary School in Evanston, Illinois.

We also work to link local farmers and consumers, increase awareness of local foods, promote the sustainable use of land, and support artisan producers, slow restaurants, and small family farms.
