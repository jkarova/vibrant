Welcome to Slow Food Chicago!

 We are a diverse group of people joined by a common interest in the gastronomic wonders of honest food and honoring our culture, traditions, environment, and community. In short, we strive to use our taste buds as guides to a better life.

We organize many fun activities: neighborhood walks, food and wine tastings, workshops, cooking demonstrations, and educational projects in schools, including the "Edible Schoolyard" project at the Dawes Elementary School in Evanston, IL. We also work to link local farmers and consumers, increase awareness of local foods, provide food education to children and adults, promote the sustainable use of land, and support artisan producers, slow restaurants, and small family farms.

You can find out more about our activities in the Gallery of Past Activities and the Calendar.
